About the Project

Why We Built This

Autism Spectrum Disorder affects an estimated 1 in 65 children in India, yet diagnosis typically arrives at age 3–5 — well past the point where early intervention does the most good. Conventional diagnosis is slow, specialist-dependent, and inaccessible to many families.

This project explores whether a webcam-grade eye-tracking heatmap, combined with a two-stage deep learning pipeline, can offer an objective, accessible, pre-clinical screening signal — not a replacement for clinical diagnosis, but a low-cost first flag that could shorten the path to a specialist.
