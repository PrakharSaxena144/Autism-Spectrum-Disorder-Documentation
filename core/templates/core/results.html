What Worked

  • CLAHE consistently improved performance in every pipeline
  • Bilateral filter paired well with CLAHE — preserved edges, reduced noise
  • Skipping the final binarisation step (Pipeline 3) outperformed binarising

What Hurt Performance

  • Watershed segmentation fragmented fixation blobs — accuracy fell to 65.38% at that stage
  • Stacking too many sequential operations (Pipeline 1) caused information loss
  • Excess morphology/adaptive thresholding lowered precision and recall together
